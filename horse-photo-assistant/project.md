# Horse Photo Assistant

## Vision

Horse Photo Assistant helps riders, trainers, breeders, and photographers capture better horse portraits.

Many horse photos are ruined because the horse's ears are not forward at the moment the photo is taken.

The goal of the app is to increase the percentage of usable photos by combining attention-grabbing sounds with automated photo capture.

The app should feel like a professional equestrian photography tool rather than a generic camera app.

---

## Problem

Horse owners often struggle to get horses to:

* Put their ears forward
* Look alert
* Focus on the camera
* Maintain the expression long enough for a good photograph

Photographers currently use:

* Whinnies
* Feed bucket noises
* Plastic bags
* Squeakers
* Human assistants

These methods are inconsistent and require perfect timing.

---

## Solution

Horse Photo Assistant automates the process.

Workflow:

1. User positions horse.
2. User selects a sound.
3. User presses "Get Ears Forward".
4. Sound plays.
5. App automatically captures burst photos.
6. User reviews results.

The app removes timing pressure from the photographer.
